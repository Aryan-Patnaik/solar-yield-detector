Solar Yield Detector is an AI-powered solar monitoring platform that turns raw sunlight data into actionable insights — helping homeowners and businesses maximize their return on every solar panel.

🎯 Mission & Vision
🌍
Our Mission
To democratize solar intelligence — giving every solar panel owner access to the same data-driven tools that were once only available to large-scale utilities. We believe smarter monitoring leads to cleaner energy and better financial returns.
🔭
Our Vision
A future where every rooftop solar system runs at peak efficiency, every panel is cleaned at exactly the right time, and every rupee invested in solar energy delivers its maximum possible return — powered by AI and real-time data.

💡 What We Stand For
🔓
Free & Open Data
We use NASA POWER and Open-Meteo — both free, public APIs — because we believe solar intelligence shouldn't be locked behind expensive subscriptions.
🎯
Accuracy First
Every prediction is backed by real meteorological data and validated ML models — not guesswork. We surface uncertainty ranges so you always know how confident the model is.
🌱
Sustainability
By helping you maximize yield and reduce waste, we're directly contributing to India's clean energy goals. Every optimized panel is one step toward a carbon-neutral grid.
🔒
Privacy by Design
Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or sell personal information. All user data is stored locally and is only used to power your dashboard.
